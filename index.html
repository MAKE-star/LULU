The $100B Opportunity

Africa's informal economy employs 80% of the workforce, yet lacks trust, verification, and financial inclusion.

The Problem

  • No way to verify if a worker is trustworthy
  • Cash payments = no financial history
  • Safety risks for both parties
  • No access to credit or banking

The LULU Solution

  • Identity verification + skill certification
  • Secure escrow payments (The Handshake‚Ñ¢)
  • GPS tracking + SOS safety button
  • Trust Score = Credit Score for banking
